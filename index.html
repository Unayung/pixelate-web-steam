Frequently Asked Questions
How do I play multiplayer?

Select "VS Mode" from the main menu, then choose to create a game or join an existing lobby.

What are mercenaries?

In VS Mode, you can send enemies to your opponent's castle by spending gold on mercenaries.

How do tower upgrades work?

After each wave, choose from 3 random upgrade cards to enhance your towers, gain resources, or repair your castle.
